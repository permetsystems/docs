# God Protocol

The God Protocol is the long-term end state: participant-owned apparel brands with full on-chain supply — design, production, distribution, and profit on transparent, programmatic infrastructure.

## Concept

Traditional brands centralize ownership, obscure supply chains, and capture creative IP. The God Protocol inverts that:

- Design registered on-chain as forkable, attributable assets.
- Production documented and auditable at each step.
- Profit distributed autonomously to contributors.
- Community governs collections, treasuries, and protocol evolution.

Supply — not marketing — becomes the source of truth.

## Path

The protocol does not arrive in one step. It advances through:

1. Open fashion blocks and forkable garment libraries.
2. Curated registries and split-based profit redistribution.
3. Open production data and decentralized producer networks.
4. Human-rights and environmental oracles for verified ethical production.
5. Full on-chain supply — the God Protocol end state.

Participation spans product testing bounties, community curation, physical production infrastructure, and protocol development — with treasuries intended to return value to the communities that supply cultural and material context.
